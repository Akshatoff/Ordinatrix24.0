Snap@trix

Eligibilty: 6th to 12th

Number of Participants: 3

Mode: Offline

Aeromodelling is a creative hobby where a child learns the basics of flying with an objective of designing, building and flying the models airplane, helicopter & rockets. These models can be scaled to real planes or helicopters, or are specially designed aircraft to fly and do aerobatics. In this event students will design their own gliders with the help of chuck gliders kit and then taking a flight.

First Task -:

  1. In this round, the team will be given a kit and a manual through which they will shape their aircraft.
  2. The maximum time allotted will be 45 minutes
  3. Points will be awarded based on the designs,altitude and sustain flight, etc
  4. Teams unable to complete this task or having low scores will be eliminated

Second Task -:

  1. Students will have to fly their aircrafts from a point.
  2. Marking will be done according to the projection of aircraft and landing.
  3. Two chances will be given to fly their gliders.
