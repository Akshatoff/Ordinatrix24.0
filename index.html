It all started 19 years ago.

A fusion of creativity, talent and skill is what Ordin@trix is all about. Ordin@trix has evolved through the years moving from an interschool festival at the state level to an international event. Over the years we have had students from China, Muscat, Japan and other countries as regular participants.

PYROTECH members are

an elite team of student computer enthusiasts. They have represented the school in various competitions and won laurels in events such as web designing, software development, image editing, movie making, 3-D modelling, Robotics, photography and others.

    Guidelines

    1. ONLINE registrations will be done on a first come first served basis. No registration will be taken on the day of the event. Registrations will close on Saturday 15 April '23 by 12 noon.

    2. You can register directly through the link given below:
    https://bit.ly/ordin23registration
    Each school must ensure that a student participates in only one offline event and that they register only one team per event.

    3. Theme for this year will be VerTech - Green Technology

    4. For all submission-based events, the entries must be submitted at the respective submission links shared along with the prompt.

    5. Participants must report to the school for the event between 8 AM to 8:30 AM. If a school reaches the venue late, timely information should be sent to the organizers. Each team must be accompanied by a teacher.

    6. All future correspondence will be done through Discord and Email only.

    7. The result of prelims will be posted on the Ordin@trix website and on discord.

    8. Submissions must not: violate any third party rights, including, but not limited to, copyrights, trademark rights, or rights of privacy and publicity; contain defamatory statements; include threats to any person, place, business, or group; or be obscene or indecent.

    9. Please refer to the link on our discord, https://discord.io/ordin23 and/or our Ordin@trix website, https://pyrotechclub.com for the latest updates on the events.

    10. Decisions of the organizers & jury members regarding any issue related to Ordin@trix 23.0 will be FINAL AND BINDING on all schools and participants.

    11. To qualify for the OVERALL TROPHY, a school must take part in a minimum of 5 events.

    12. Join our discord server: https://discord.io/ordin23
    School website: https://www.vv.tagoreint.com/vv
    Instagram Page: https://www.instagram.com/pyrotech_tisvv/
